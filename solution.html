Our Solution

The Smart Health Monitor integrates IoT-enabled water quality sensors with a digital health application. The system continuously measures vital water parameters such as pH, turbidity, and chlorine level and displays them in real-time on a user-friendly dashboard. Alongside, healthcare workers or family members can manually enter health symptoms and water conditions through the data entry module.

Key Features

  • IoT sensors for real-time water quality monitoring
  • Google Charts based visualization for quick analysis
  • Alerts and notifications for abnormal readings
  • Data entry forms for manual health tracking
  • Centralized dashboard integrating both IoT and manual data

Why Our Solution?

This solution bridges the gap between environmental health data and personal healthcare monitoring. It empowers individuals, communities, and healthcare providers to make better decisions, prevent waterborne diseases, and ensure healthier living.
